Check the purity of ground oil with the help of various machine learning models. When groundnut oil adulterated with palm oil or any other substance, the readings taken from Advanced ATR-MIR Spectroscopy differs than from pure oil.

The image on the right shows a line plot between the wavelengths and their respective readings collected during spectroscopy.

X axis: Wavelengths (Å)
Y axis: Laser Spectroscopy value (Å)

Green Line: Pure oil
Red Line: Adulterated with 6.25% palm oil
Blue Line: Adulterated with 25% palm oil
Black Line: Adulterated with 50% palm oil
